Twitter Is Obsessed With Rihanna And Ross Newfound Friendship

 Posted on July 7, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  406 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

K-pop girl group Blackpink member Rosé attended the 2021 Met Gala and made history by becoming one of the first two K-pop stars to ever attend the charity event. The singer looked stunning in a chic black dress from Saint Laurent, and stepped on the red carpet alongside the fashion house’s creative director Anthony Vaccarello.

Rosé made several new friends at the annual Gala and was seen hanging out with supermodel Gigi Hadid, Good 4 U singer Olivia Rodrigo as well as Met Gala icon Rihanna at the event.

Photos of Rihanna and Rosé’s meet-cute have Twitter users obsessing over their newfound friendship, with the singers’ fans wondering if a collaboration might be coming!

Rosé Befriended Rihanna At The Met

As per a journalist at Vogue magazine, Rosé and Rihanna spoke at the Met Gala and “immediately fell in love with each other”.

The photos see Rihanna and Rosé in conversation with each other, with the K-pop star smiling from ear to ear. She reportedly attended the Met Gala After-party hosted by Rihanna in New York City, where the singers were photographed together.

“smells like queen riri and rosé collab???” a fan inquired.

“the first album she bought was Rihanna's "ANTI" album.. and now she finally met her in person..” gushed a fan.

“ROSÉ and Rihanna talking about the possible RiriPink Collab in the future?” added a fan.

Rosé also shared to Instagram a photo of the moment she met the singer, writing “The moment I died - I love you.”

The Diamonds singer wore an all-black Balenciaga ensemble at the event, complete with a black beanie.

The theme for the 2021 edition of the annual event was “In America: A Lexicon of Fashion” to celebrate American fashion.

Several celebrities stunned on the red carpet - from Kim Kardashian’s Harry Potter inspired look to AOC’s controversial “Tax the Rich” outfit and Frank Ocean’s lime-green baby doll.

The Met Gala is expected to return in 2022 once again, on the first Monday in May! Fans hope all the members of Blackpink - Jennie, Lalisa, Rosé and Jisoo can find their way to the event!
