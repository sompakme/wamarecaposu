Nicki Minaj Considers Booking Katt Williams For 'Pink Friday 2' Tour

 Posted on September 6, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  306 words  |  Aldo Pusey

NIcki Minaj is already bringing bars and ballads to her Pink Friday 2 Tour, and may just be bringing the laughs, as well.

According to the Queens rep, she’s looking to lock down Katt Williams‘ fee if he were to join her on the massive tour, which has already secured Monica as one opening act. The “Big Difference” rapper took to her Instagram Live to make the inquiry.

“I know that this is the busiest man in show business, ok? I know that he’s rich, and I know that he’s the funniest man in showbiz. Can ya’ll hit up Katt Williams and ask him what would be his price…to be a part of the Pink Friday 2 Gag City Tour,” she asked of her Live followers.

While he’s been a respected comic for years, Williams’ celebrity sky-rocketed in January thanks to his viral sit-down with Shannon Sharpe on Club Shay Shay, where he called out fellow comics such as Cedric The Entertainer, Steve Harvey, Michael Blackson, Kevin Hart, and others.

The proposition to join Minaj on tour would be a tempting one if it allows for the completion of his own current trek, The Dark Matters Tour, which recently landed both Torrei Hart and Mo’Nique as acts on the bill.

As for the Pink Friday 2 Tour, Minaj will be hitting cities ranging from Las Vegas, Orlando, and Philadelphia to London, Amsterdam and Berlin. Monica spoke of her inclusion on the tour during a recent visit to The Jennifer Hudson Show.

“I’m really grateful for Nicki, because she’s one of those people that has always said ‘Monica meant everything to my childhood,’ and some people, they act like they forget. And so, it’s pretty cool to not be forgotten and to be called for things like such, so we ‘gon show up and show out.”
