Is 'The Royal Treatment' Star Laura Marano Single?

 Posted on September 19, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  810 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

2022 has already been a very prolific year for Netflix. From mystery dramas like Harlan Coben's Stay Close, to documentaries like The Tinder Swindler, audiences have been spoilt for choice of good content. Even an old show like Manifest has found new life in this new-year wave.

Another production that has really resonated with fans is The Royal Treatment, a romantic drama film by Rick Jacobson. Starring in the picture is multi-talented actress Laura Marano, who plays a Manhattan-based hairdresser who gets the opportunity to work at a royal wedding.

The Royal Treatment is a major milestone in the career of Marano, who has had an impressive - if slightly understated - career. The actress is the younger sister of Ghost Whisperer star Vanessa Marano, one of a number of celebs who broke out on the CBS supernatural drama. Laura also once made a cameo on the show.

In The Royal Treatment, she is flanked by Aladdin star Mena Massoud, who plays a prince from the fictional country of Lavania. Their chemistry in the movie has prompted some whispers of a fling between the two. But just exactly what is the truth about Marano's current dating status?

Does Laura Marano Have A Fling With Mena Massoud?

Fans were strongly impressed by the performances of Marano and Massoud opposite each other in the film. 'The Royal Treatment is a delightful surprise, armed with a sweet story and some charming performances from its two young leads,' one fan review reads on Rotten Tomatoes.

This smooth chemistry has prompted a lot of rumors that the two could probably be an item. The truth is completely different, though. Marano and Massoud first met on set while filming the rom-com back in February 2021. They struck up a good work partnership, but that's the extent of their relationship.

In fact, Massoud has been in a long-term relationship with Emily Shah, an Indian-American actress who held the crown of Miss New Jersey in 2014. The two travel together frequently, and Shah often posts cute pictures of them together on her Instagram page.

Marano sometimes shares photos of her and Massoud goofing around behind the scenes and off set. The images may have worked to fuel the rumors and speculation of their romantic involvement, but all they showcase is the two stars having a good time at work.

Who Is Laura Marano Dating Now?

It appears that Marano tries as much as possible to keep her personal life away from public scrutiny. This is not surprising, given that it's a common thing for celebrities to keep details of their love lives under wraps.

Going by the information about Marano available in the public domain, all signs point towards the actress being currently single. Her most recent, publicly-known relationship was with R'n'B musician Thomas Macken. The two reportedly started dating in 2018, and stayed together for a period during the global COVID lockdown.

Marano - who herself is also a recording artist - would often go to Instagram to share some of their daily routines. However, that relationship appeared to peter out at some point in 2020, although reasons for any breakup remain unclear.

Macken's portfolio of work is not too shabby. He has worked on a few film soundtracks, including for the musical, Pitch Perfect.

Even after their breakup, Marano and Macken continued to collaborate, as she featured him in the remix of her song Run and also invited him to work with her on her EP, You.

Who Did Laura Marano Date In The Past?

Before her time with Macken, Marano had been in two known relationships. She was said to have briefly dated a guy called Andrew Gorin. The pair started dating in 2014, but separated a short time after. There is very little information known about who Gorin is, including the reasons for their breakup.

Between 2010 and 2011, Marano was involved with another musician - pop star Ross Lynch. The Colorado-born artist shares similar interests as Marano, seeing as he is also an actor. The two of them connected while they were playing Austin Moon and Ally Dawson respectively, in the Disney comedy series Austin & Ally.

The show effectively gave Lynch his breakout role as an actor, resulting in four Teen Choice Awards between 2014 and 2016. In music, he was the lead vocalist of the pop-rock band R5. Currently, he headlines another band - the Driver Era (TDE) - alongside his brother, Rocky Lynch.

As was the case with Gorin and Macken, the reasons for Marano's breakup with Lynch remain unclear. It is also not known whether the pair have stayed in contact through the years, or not.
