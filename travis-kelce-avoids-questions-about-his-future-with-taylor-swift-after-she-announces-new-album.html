Travis Kelce Refused To Answer A Question About His Future With Taylor Swift

Questions surrounding Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce's relationship are always going around, as fans adore them together and would love to see their love story last for a long time. As much as they understand and appreciate their fans' support, it's understandable that the power couple would get tired of having their private business be public discussion. Right now, they are both going through very important moments in their careers, with Taylor recently becoming the only artist in history to win the Album of the Year Grammy Award four times, and Travis getting ready to take the Chiefs to the Superbowl.

Recently, during a press conference, Travis Kelce was asked, not for the first time, about his future with Taylor Swift. Rumors have been going around about the couple getting engaged in the Superbowl, and he was asked point-blank if he was planning to propose. "Is there going to be another ring besides the Super Bowl ring if you win this thing on Sunday?" a reporter questioned, to which the NFL player replied, "I’m focused on getting this ring, and that’s all that my mind’s focused on right now."

Travis Heard The New Taylor Swift Album Already - And He Couldn't Be Prouder

Travis Kelce couldn't be prouder of how Taylor Swift is making history, and he's constantly in awe of everything she does. In the same press conference, he was asked about her triumphant night at the Grammys, and he couldn't help gushing, "She’s unbelievable. She’s rewriting the history books herself."

He also opened up about how her staggering success puts pressure on him to keep up with her . "I told her I'll have to hold up my end of the bargain and come home with some hardware too."

He's not only proud of Taylor Swift's awards and record-breaking success, though. He's also incredibly happy about her new record, The Tortured Poets Department, and admitted that he's one of the few people who have had the privilege of having listened to some of the album already. The expectations are incredibly high, and according to Travis, they will absolutely be met. He said the album is "unbelievable," and that he "can’t wait for her to shake up the world when it finally drops."

Fans shouldn't worry about their favorite celebrity couple. Although it seems like it's been a long time, the truth is they have been together for less than a year. It's only natural that they wouldn't want to share so many details of their love life, but that doesn't mean they aren't going strong.
