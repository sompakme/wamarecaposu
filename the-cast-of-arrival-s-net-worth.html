Arrival is probably one of the best alien sci-fi flicks out there to date, and it certainly got noticed by critics and even the Academy. With 8 Oscar nominations (and 1 win), this drama mystery follows a linguist who is hired by the military to communicate with an alien species - who have mysteriously landed 12 spacecrafts around the world.

Despite the utter brilliance of this stunning film, it also has an impeccable and pretty A-list cast. In case you're wondering which one of these actors could probably afford their own spaceship, we've made a list. Here is the cast of this incredible sci-fi flick and their net worth (according to Celebrity Net Worth).

Christian Jadah - $500,000

Christian Jadah's net worth isn't super flashy, but this upcoming actor is certainly going to be coming onto our radar soon. He actually already has a few dozen acting credits, but most of them are small television roles or supporting movie characters.

Still, you might remember him as Private Combs in Arrival, but he also had small roles in X-Men: Days of Future Past, Quantico, The Hummingbird Project, and Separated at Birth. Clearly, he's been in television and film, and this man is a pretty regular background actor. Of course, we expect to see him on the front lines soon.

Mark O'Brien - $1 Million

This young actor is currently breaking open the doors to Hollywood. While he was Captain Marks in this sci-fi flick, he's actually been a part of some huge projects since then. You might recognize this face from some recent blockbuster films, including The Darkest Minds, Bad Times at the El Royale, Ready or Not, and Marriage Story.

He also has a couple more movies in the works, so we expect that his breakout is yet to come. Still, at this point in his career, $1 million is nothing to sneer at, and we're sure his net worth is only about to climb.

Frank Schorpion - $2 Million

This man was Dr. Kettler in this alien movie, but he has over 100 acting credits to show for his career. Of course, most of these are also one-time television show appearances or small movie roles. Still, you might recognize him most recently from The Hummingbird Project (alongside our friend, Christian!), Pet Sematary, and Good Sam. 

This man is no newbie, and this net worth shows for about three decades of small acting roles. He even has three in the works, so we're sure we'll see more of this man before he's done in Hollywood.

Michael Stuhlbarg - $3 Million

Michael Stuhlbarg played Agent Halpern in Arrival, but it turns out that he's actually been in quite a few Oscar-nominated flicks. Again, mainly secondary roles, but you've definitely seen his face in the The Shape of Water, The Post, and Call Me by Your Name. In fact, he was even in Doctor Strange!

However, most of this net worth probably comes from his television acting, where he had a recurring role in Boardwalk Empire, Fargo, The Looming Tower, and Traitors. He's actually been a part of some seriously famous projects, and we're sure that net worth is far from its peak.

Forest Whitaker - $40 Million

Forest Whitaker is quite a legend in the industry, and he played Colonel Weber in this sci-fi drama. While he has over 100 acting credits under his belt, it's not surprising his net worth is as high at is. If you're wondering what his most recent projects are, you'll probably recognize this famous face from Black Panther, City of Lies, and Rogue One: A Star Wars Story. 

Surprisingly, this actor actually has a lot of television experience, too, and most recently had a recurring role in Empire and Godfather of Harlem. He's not done acting yet, and we expect much more from this talented man.

Jeremy Renner - $50 Million

Jeremy Renner co-starred with Amy Adams in this flick as Ian Donnelly. Of course, we probably know this man best as Clint Barton (AKA Hawkeye) from the Avengers franchise. Obviously, that's likely where most of this man's dough comes from. Still, Jeremy Renner certainly doesn't get enough credit for his versaility, and we suggest checking out American Hustle and Wind River for some brilliant films.

Of course, he's also pretty prominent in action flicks (including Mission Impossible and the Bourne franchise), and you'll definitely see him in the new Hawkeye television series from Disney+. We love this man, and this movie was certainly better with him in it!

Amy Adams - $60 Million

Amy Adams was perfect for the role of Louise Banks, but she continues to blow us away time and time again. She's actually been nominated for 6 Oscars, so, it's not surprising her net worth is as impressive as it is. She was actually also in American Hustle alongside Jeremy Renner, and more critically-acclaimed films include Nocturnal Animals, Her, and Vice. 

In fact, she's also dabbled in the superhero world, but joined DC's side as Lois Lane, Superman's leading lady. You can also catch this bombshell on television, where she played the lead role in Sharp Objects. Amy Adams is a versatile and brilliant actress, and we certainly can't see what's yet to come from this incredible woman.

Tzi Ma - $65 Million

If you didn't know this man's name, you've certainly seen his face. He plays General Shang in this sci-fi flick, but he has over 100 acting credits that have helped create his impressive net worth. He's certainly a legendary actor, and most recently you'd probably recognize him from Skyscraper, Bad Company, and The Farewell. 

However, he's actually a pretty regular television actor, including many one-time roles, but also recurring ones in The Man in the High Castle, Silicon Valley and Wu Assassins. In fact, you can catch this talented man in the upcoming live-action Mulan. That net worth will only be growing, and of that we're pretty confident!
