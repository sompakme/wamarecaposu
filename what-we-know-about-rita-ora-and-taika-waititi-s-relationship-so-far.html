As the fun of this newly discovered connection continues to spiral, we break down what we know about Rita Ora and Taika Waititi's relationship so far.

Rita then went ahead and captioned the post "Good times, Memories, random things on my phone and the one I love." That's a pretty romantic tag for a photo and is bound to get the attention of fans. Rita went ahead to hashtag it "#Midweekupdate."

In a recent photo, we see Rita Ora along with Christian Bale, Isla Fisher, and a few others having dinner. 6 Rita And Taika Spotted Again

You can only get spotted a couple of times together before people begin to figure out what's going on. Once again Rita Ora and Taika were seen together in Sydney at the world premiere of Rupaul's Drag Race. There is a thing between the two because it is glaring. However, neither Rita Ora nor Taika has made any official statements on the subject.

We have seen pictures, videos, and all sorts of things regarding Rita and Taika but what we have not seen is the pair step out together as a duo publicly. Well, that is until now. The singer and the director were first seen together as a duo Rita's Prospero Tequila Barbecue Celebration on July 4th.

The pair gladly took pictures together openly as they rocked some ravishing outfits. Rita appeared in a pink and gold dress while Taika appeared in a coordinated mixture of black, white, red and blue. Black being his trousers and the remaining colors his shirt.
