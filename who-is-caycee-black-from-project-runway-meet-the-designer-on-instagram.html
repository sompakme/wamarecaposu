Caycee Black, a designer, has been hitting the headlines after she was confirmed to be one of the contestants of the 19th season of hit reality show Project Runway.

Winner of Best New Designer Awards, Caycee Black, is set to set the stage on fire when she enters as a contestant on the 19th season of Project Runway.

You can watch the premiere of the show on 14trh of October on Bravo.

Caycee Black Wikipedia: Who Is She?

Caycee Black is a fashion designer who was born and raised in Housten, Texas. She is the daughter of Dr. Ken Black. The artist grew up around paint and denim. Needless to say, her path was set from an early age.

Indeed, she proved everyone’s expectations right when she moved to new york to study at Parsons School of Design. Here, she got her BFA in design.

She set up her shop in NYC and conjured up experiences working at Anna Sui, Club Monaco, Tibi. Her designs like herself were playful, fun, whimsical, and very much rooted.

She knew she made it big when she styled notable celebrities like Drew Barrymore, Michelle Williams, Lilly Singh, Regina Spektor, and Solange Knowles.

Caycee Black Boyfriend: Is She Dating Someone?

Caycee Black is not dating as of now. She is indeed enjoying life as a single woman.

From her Instagram, we had speculated that she must be flirting with her model Patrick Clark when she captioned the picture with the saying, ” You make my heart smile.” But after a quick search, we discovered that he is in a relationship with another beautiful lady.

Although love has not come knocking on her door right now, we have no doubt she would find romance soon.

Meet Caycee Black On Instagram

You can follow her personal Instagram and be part of her 3.8k followers. It seems that Caycee does not like being famous as she has kept her account private.

You can find her pictures along with her designs on her professional Instagram account called caycee_black.

Through this social media, we can get an idea of her personality as she has meticulously curated and color-coordinated all her Instagram posts to create a beautiful layout.

Caycee Black Net Worth: How Rich Is She?

The net worth of Caycee Black is estimated to be around 1 million to 5 million dollars.

Black makes and spends her millions by being a professional high-end fashion designer. She started her career in 2002 when she worked as a design assistant for Anna Sui. When the year 2009 hit, she had already worked as a designer in many high-end designer brands.

Caycee finally got her big break when she was promoted to be the senior designer on Coach and Later at Tina Stephens.

Finally, in December of 2012, she had enough experience to open her own clothing company called Caycee Black Inc in New York City.
