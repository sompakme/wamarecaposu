Who Is Melanie Lynskey’s Ex-Husband Jimmi Simpson?

Jimmi Simpson is an American actor born on November 21, 1975, in Hackettstown, New Jersey. He is best known for his roles in TV and film, notably as William in the sci-fi TV show Westworld, House of Cards, and Black Mirror. 

Simpson took his first acting class in Hackettstown High School, after which he pursued BA in Theater at Bloomsburg University.

There, he acted for four seasons at the Williamstown Theatre Festival in Williamstown, Massachusetts.

He landed his first film role as Noah in the 2000 romantic comedy Loser, directed by Amy Heckerling.

Simpson was married to Two and a Half Men star Melanie Lynskey in 2007, and the couple split 7 years later.

Jimmi Simpson Net Worth

Simpson makes a net worth estimated at $6 million from his acting and producing career in film.

Jimmi Simpson Wife

Simpson is currently known to be single, after having married twice as of 2022.

Following his split with Melanie in 2014, Simpson married English actress Sophia Del Pizzo in April 2019.

The pair eventually split, based on a July 2021 announcement attributing the filing for divorce to Simpson.
