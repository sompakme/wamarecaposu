Jessica Biel became a household name when she was one of the stars of the TV show 7th Heaven when she was still a teenager. Since then, the public saw her career take off, and she starred in several movies. Biel also made headlines thanks to her personal life, especially when she started dating Justin Timberlake, her husband since 2012.

According to Celebrity Networth, Jessica Biel has a fortune evaluated at $18,000,000. The Sinner star is clearly used to a fairy tale lifestyle, that includes amazing houses and holidays most of us can just dream about. Keep scrolling and discover how Jessica Biel spends her millions.

Tribeca Apartment - $20,000,000

Jessica Biel and Justin Timberlake bought a new home in Tribeca, one of the most exclusive regions in New York, in 2017. They paid $20,000,000 for the place, $7,000,000 less than the initial price, according to Money.

The 5,400 square-foot house has four bedrooms and six bathrooms. There are luxurious features, like a rooftop protected from the paparazzi eyes. Inside the home, the family can also enjoy an inside swimming pool and a Turkish bath. It does look like the perfect place for an A-list couple and their children.

SoHo Apartment - $6,567,713

In 2010, Jessica and her then-boyfriend Justin Timberlake bought an apartment in SoHo for $6,567,713, a bargain if compared with the previous item on this list. The 2,500 square-foot apartment has three bedrooms and 3.5 bathrooms. The floor-to-ceiling windows offer people a magnificent view of Manhattan. There is also a terrace that is perfect for enjoying the summer or some wine in the evening.

According to The Daily Mail, they sold the place in 2018, just after buying their new home in New York. Although they asked over $7,000,000, the final price remained around $6,000,000.

Handbag Collection

Having a collection with design bags are an essential item for wealthy celebrities. Jessica Biel is not an exception. The actress was spotted wearing a Louis Vuitton limited edition purse that costs $4,000 and a Gucci handbag that costs $3,000. It's money enough to pay a rental.

Biel is a fan of high-design bags, and she also wears brands like Chanel, Fendi, and Oscar de la Renta. The actress seems to have a handbag collection that is worth at least thousands of dollars — a dream for many women out there.

Personal Trainer

For many celebrities, it is common to have a personal trainer, which is a high investment. One of the persons responsible for Biel's fantastic body is the personal Jason Walsh, who is very popular among celebrities like Matt Damon and Mandy Moore.

The actress likes intensive training, and it is not a surprise she is among the celebrities who swear by CrossFit. Biel tries to exercise at least three times a week. She already shared a video where she does "treadmill training, bench press reps, explosive leg weights, stomach crunches, and kickboxing moves," as Daily Star describes.

Wedding - $6,500,000

When Jessica Biel and Justin Timberlake tied the knot in 2012, many sources affirmed they spent approximately $6,500,000 on their big day. They never confirmed the rumors, but it was indeed an expensive night.

They also paid the tickets for the guests, who just discovered their destination a few days before traveling. It was indeed a night the couple and the guests will never forget.

Wedding Dress - $100,000

Jessica Alba and her husband spent an incredible amount on their wedding. So it is no surprise she also wore a super expensive dress to walk down the aisle. Giambattista Valli designed the exclusive pink dress that rumors say have cost $100,000 and is often listed as one of the most expensive wedding dresses ever.

Honeymoon

Of course, after a fairytale wedding that costs millions, Jessica Biel and Justin would choose a fantastic destination for their honeymoon. And they would not go cheap on their honeymoon.

Wine

Jessica Biel appreciates the finest things in life, and she has the money to afford it. One of her pleasures in life is to taste good wine. For her wedding, she hired a winemaker friend to create a unique wine just for the occasion. Jesse Katz, a winemaker and close friend of the couple, came up with the Blue Ocean Floorwine. According to Katz, he knew the couple for a long time, and he knew what they would love. Vacations

One of the pros of being an A-list celebrity is that you don't need to think about saving money for holidays. We often see Jessica Biel traveling the world with her family.

In 2018, she followed Justin Timberlake during his tour in Europe. Together with their son, they had the chance to enjoy France, The Netherlands, and Sweden. She shared many images on her social media. This year they decided to go to Europe again and went to Tuscany. Of course, both travel very hard and deserve this fantastic time off.

Hair Stylist

Another important point on a celebrity item is to have the perfect hairdresser. For Jessica Biel, this person is Adir Abergel, who is also responsible for the hair of other celebrities like Jennifer Garner and Anne Hathaway. Having A-list celebrities among their clients helps any professional to reach a high status and charge equally high prices.

Abergel, who is also the creative director at Virtue, already gave some interviews sharing some tips about how to have Jessica Biel perfect wave hair. He said that her hair has a perfect texture, which helps a lot in his work.
