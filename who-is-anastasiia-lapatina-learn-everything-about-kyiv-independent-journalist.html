Who Is Anastasiia Lapatina?

Anastasiia Lapatina is a journalist. During Russia’s invasion of Ukraine this week, a Ukrainian journalist turned down an interview with Russia Today, also known as RT, accusing it of doing “nothing but disseminating hazardous misinformation.”

She released screenshots of her response to the Russian network on her Twitter account on Wednesday, February 23.

“Got an email from RT today,” Anastasiia wrote in the tweet’s title.

Anastasiia replied “I do not and will not ever appear on Russian media networks, as they hold huge responsibility for the war in my country, which has left 14,000 Ukrainians dead,” in feedback to an email from someone identifying as James.

“RT, in particular, has done nothing except disseminating hazardous falsehoods,” she continued, before signing off with “Best, Anastasiia.”
