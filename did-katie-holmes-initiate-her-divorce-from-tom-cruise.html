Tom Cruise And Katie Holmes Were Married In Italy

Tom Cruise and Katie Holmes wedding was held at the Castello Orsini-Odescalchi in Bracciano, Italy in November 2006. Seven months later, their daughter Suri was born on April 18, 2006. The star-studded ceremony was held at Odescalchi Castle in Italy on November 18, 2006.

The nuptials were attended by some of the biggest names in Hollywood, including the likes of Victoria Beckham, Jennifer Lopez, John Travolta, and former Scientologist, Leah Remini.
