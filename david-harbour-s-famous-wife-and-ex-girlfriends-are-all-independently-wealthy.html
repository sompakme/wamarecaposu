What Is David Harbour's Ex Maria Thayer Worth?

Years before David Harbour became a major movie and television star, his acting career started to take off during the 2000s. At that point in Harbour's life, he met another young actor on the rise named Maria Thayer.

According to Elle, Harbour and Thayer were a couple from 2009 until 2011.

While Thayer has never become a household name, she is the kind of actor that people recognize even if they can't figure out from where.

Best known for starring in the show Strangers with Candy, Thayer has also popped up in lots of other series like Superstore, Eagleheart, and Comedy Bang! Bang! On the film side of things, Thayer appeared in movies like Forgetting Sarah Marshall, Accepted, and Hitch.

According to idolnetworth.com, Thayer currently has a $700,000 fortune. While that figure should be taken with a grain of salt since that website isn't the most credible, it does give a ballpark idea of how much Thayer is worth.

What Is Alison Sudol Worth?

According to whosdatedwho.com, David Harbour dated Alison Sudol from the year 2017 until 2019. Like a lot of celebrity relationships, it is hard to know exactly when Harbour and Sudol became a couple but Elle reports they went public with their relationship in 2019.

A singer and musician who goes by the name A Fine Frenzy, Sudol's music has appeared in several TV shows and movies. More notably, Sudol has enjoyed a great deal of success as an actor.

Back in 2014, Sudol landed a recurring role in the acclaimed series Transparent. While that was a major moment in Sudol's career, it was in 2016 that things really took off for her.

Hired to star in the Harry Potter spin-off Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them, many fans greatly enjoyed Sudol's portrayal of Queenie Goldstein. Then in 2018 and 2022, Sudol returned to the character in Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald and Fantastic Beasts: The Secrets of Dumbledore.

According to networthcelebrities.com, Sudol has an estimated $2 million fortune. Just like Maria Thayer's entry, that number should be taken with a grain of salt since networthcelebrities.com isn't the most credible website.

How Much Money Is Lily Allen Worth?

After David Harbour and Alison Sudol went their separate ways, he began dating the singer Lily Allen who is a decade younger than him in 2019. Evidently very happy together, it didn't take Harbour and Allen long to commit to one another.

In 2020, Harbour and Allen tied the knot during a very unorthodox but fun-looking wedding, and they've been together ever since.

A very successful singer-songwriter, Allen has released several hit songs over the years. Most notably, Allen is best known for songs like "5 O'Clock", "Smile", "True Love", " The Fear", and "F*ck You" among others.

Thanks to all of Allen's hit singles and the money she has made touring, she has a $4 million fortune according to celebritynetworth.com.

What Is Julia Stiles's Net Worth?

A couple who dated for just over two years in the early 2010s, David Harbour and Julia Stiles reportedly were together from 2011 until 2013.

At the height of Stiles' career, she was one of the most in-demand teen actors in the business. First made famous when she starred in 10 Things I Hate About You, Stiles then went on to headline the extremely popular film Save the Last Dance.

On top of her two most famous roles, Stiles has also appeared in movies like the Bourne franchise, Mona Lisa Smile, The Prince & Me, Silver Linings Playbook, and Orphan: First Kill. Stiles also starred in a season of the show Dexter, and she is currently headlining an Amazon Original series called The Lake.

Highly successful during her acting career, Stiles has managed to accumulate a $12 million fortune according to celebritynetworth.com.
