People who engage in professional sporting activities, often earn a lot of money especially when they are playing in leagues that are top tier. It doesn't matter the type of sport or the role they play in it, if the league is one of the highest ranked, they will definitely get to earn a lot of money. In American sports, the NFL is the highest ranked, closely followed by the NBA. In fact, the NFL spends twice as much money on players than the NBA does as they garner more revenue annually. In this list, we bring to you the top 10 NFL coaches who earn the most salary per year.

Sean Michael McDermott ($8 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Sean Michael McDermott is an American coach, 2 times NFC champion who currently coaches the Buffalo Bills of the National Football League since 2017. His NFL coaching career began in 2001 as an assistant coach for the Philadelphia Eagles, he then became the secondary coach of the team in 2007. 2011 saw him becoming the defensive coordinator for Carolina Panthers. He currently earns $8 million per annum.

Andy Reid ($8 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Another highest-paid NFL coach on the list is the American coach Andy Reid who currently earns $8 million per annum, his coaching career started in 1982 as a Graduate assistant for BYU. He was also the head coach for Philadelphia Eagles at some points in his career and he is currently coaching the Kansas City Chiefs team in the NFL from 2013 till date.

Mike Tomlin ($8 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Michael Pettaway Tomlin A.K.A. Mike Tomlin started his coaching career in 1995 at Virginia Military Institute as an assistant coach under head coach Bill Stewart. His coaching career has seen him leading his team to 10 playoff runs, 3 AFC Championship Games, 7 division titles, a title in Super Bowl XLIII, and 2 Super Bowl appearances in Pittsburgh Steelers of NFL. He won the Super Bowl at age 36, which made him the youngest head coach to win that and this record was broken by Sean McVay in Super Bowl LVI. It's little wonder why heâ€™s on this list with earnings of $8 million per annum because he holds the longest streak in the NFL as he has never lost a game in his 15 seasons as head coach.

Matt Rhule ($8.5 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Mathew Kenneth Rhule also known as Matt Rhule is a former player who was most recently head coach of the Carolina Panthers of the NFL from 2020-2022. His coaching career started in 1998 as a Volunteer Assistant at Penn State, he was once a head coach for Temple, Baylor, and finally, Carolina Panthers before he was fired in October 2022. Before he was fired, he earned $8.5 million per year and was once Big 12 Coach of 2019 and AP Big 12 coach of the year in 2019. He was replaced by Steven Wilks who is presently the interim coach and currently earns $8.5 million too.

Frank Reich ($9 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Frank Michael Reich Jr. also known as Frank Reich was a quarterback for Buffalo Bills, Carolina Panthers, New York Jets, and Detroit Lions between 1985 - 1998. His coaching career started in 2006 as a Coach Intern for Indianapolis Colts before he became head coach Indianapolis Colts and he earned $9 million until he was fired on November 7th, 2022.

John Harbaugh ($9 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

John William Harbaugh was also once a Philadelphia Eagles coach who currently coaches the Baltimore Ravens as head coach with an annual salary of $9 million. His coaching career started as far back as 1984 in Western Michigan, he has coached a number of teams including Cincinnati, Pittsburgh, and Indiana, and for about 9 years in Philadelphia Eagles. In 2008, he joined Baltimore Ravens, he was once Super Bowl Champion, AP NFL Coach of the Year in 2019, and PFWA coach of 2019.

Kylie Shanahan ($9.5 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

More would definitely be expected from the son of former Denver Broncos and Washington Redskins head coach Mike Shanahan and it is no wonder why he is on the list of the top 10 highest NFL coaches in the world. The current head coach of the San Francisco 49ers of the NFL currently earns $9.5 million per annum. He started his coaching career in 2003 as a graduate assistant at UCLA. In 2016, he won the AP NFL Assistant coach of the year, and he led the San Francisco 49ers to Super Bowl LIV making him and his father the first father-son head coaching duo to reach Super Bowl.

Bill Belichick ($12.5 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Being the general manager of the New England Patriots has earned him not just extensive authority over the New England Patriots' football operations, but an annual salary of $12.5 million. As head coach, Bill Belichick holds numerous coaching records such as 6 times Super Bowl Champion, 3 times AP NFL Coach of the Year, etc. His coaching career started in 1975 in Baltimore Colts as Special Assistant and he was once the assistant head and defensive coach for New York Jets.
