Minecraft streamer and content creator Technoblade has passed away at age 23 from cancer. His death comes nearly a year after he announced his cancer diagnosis.

Technoblade’s death was announced by his father via a YouTube video titled “so long nerds” on Thursday, June 30, 2022.

In the highly emotional video, Technoblade’s father read a farewell message from his late son;

Technoblade’s father also revealed that his late son wrote his farewell message to his fans approximately eight hours before passing on.

In the video, Technoblade, who has kept his real name a secret revealed that he is called Alex.
