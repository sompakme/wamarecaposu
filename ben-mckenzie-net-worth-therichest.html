Ben McKenzie has a net worth that is estimated to be at $13 million. Born on September 12, 1978, in Texas, he is a renowned actor and producer. He has starred in the hit series, The O.C and is currently a star in TNT's series, Southland.

Born to a family of artists, his mother was a prize winning poet while his nephew is an award winning playwright. The University of Virginian graduate has been an active actor and producer since 2000.

Ben Mckenzie rose to fame in 2005 after featuring in the Fox premiered television series The O.C. The show was an instant success and Mckenzie went on to earn the Choice Breakout TV Star-Male among other nominations. With this success, McKenzie started being featured in magazines and was ranked No. 5 in the list of "100 Sexiest Men Alive". He has also featured in other debut films such as Junebug, 88 Minutes and Snakes On A Plane among many other movies. In 2008, his solo performance of Dalton Trumbo's novel, Johnny Got His Gun, earned him the much needed critical acclaim while in 2009 featured in The Eight Percent, a short movie which won the Delta Air lines Fly-in Movie Contest.
