American TV personality Ross Matthews has an estimated net worth of $1 million. Starting out as an intern, Matthews later became a correspondent for The Tonight Show with Jay Leno, where he was known as "Ross the Intern". He has also appeared on other TV shows such as The Insider, Chelsea Lately and Celebrity Fit Club. Currently, he is seen on After Lately, which is aired on E! Network. Since September 6, 2013, he has been hosting the interactive talk show Hello Ross.

Ross Matthews was born on September 24, 1979 in Mount Vernon, Washington, USA. He graduated from University of La Verne majoring in Communications. He appeared on shows like  Interior Therapy with Jeff Lewis, The View, Days of our Lives, Theoui Howard Stern Show, and Love or Hate. 

While working for The Tonight Show with Jay Leno, he was able to cover several important events, such as the Academy Awards, movie premieres, and two Winter Olympics Games. He previously worked for E! Network, and has covered the E! Live From the Red Carpet coverage of the 2010 Emmy Awards, the 2011 Academy Awards, the 2011 Golden Globe Awards  and 2011 Screen Actors Guild Awards.
