Biography Timeline

Indiana Massara is an Australian singer, actress, and social media personality known for her multi-talented career and engaging online presence. Born on August 23, 2002, in Perth, Australia, Massara began her career as a model before transitioning to acting and music. She gained popularity on social media platforms like TikTok and YouTube before landing roles in television and film.

  • August 23, 2002: Indiana Massara was born in Perth, Australia.
  • 2013: Massara begins her modelling career, working with brands such as Mattel and Kmart.
  • 2015: Massara moves to Los Angeles, California, to pursue acting and music.
  • 2016: Massara gains a following on the social media app Musical.ly, now known as TikTok, with her lip-syncing and dance videos.
  • 2017: Massara’s acting debut in the Australian television series “Neighbours.”
  • 2018: Massara lands a recurring role in the American television series “Chicken Girls” and releases her debut single “Drama.”
  • 2019: Massara releases the singles “Apology” and “In Your Dreams” and stars in the film “A Cinderella Story: Christmas Wish.”
  • 2020: Massara releases the singles “Do It Like That” and “Breathe,” and collaborates with Puerto Rican singer and rapper Lenny Tavarez on the single “Quiero Volver.”
  • 2021: Massara releases the singles “Waste My Time” and “Smoke In My Eyes” and announces her upcoming EP “Ruin.”

Indiana Massara has proven herself a multi-talented artist with a bright future in the entertainment industry. She has built a dedicated fanbase through her engaging online presence and has used her platform to advocate for causes such as mental health awareness. Massara’s music blends elements of pop and R&B, and fans and industry insiders alike highly anticipate her upcoming EP.
