How Did Ashley Graham Meet Justin Ervin?

Ashley Graham and Justin Ervin tied the knot in 2010, only a year following their first encounter. During an interview with InStyle UK, Graham revealed that she actually met Justin in quite a surprising way. The two first crossed paths when Ashley was volunteering at her church on 'Porn Sunday'.

Graham shared that the two had met "in church for 'Porn Sunday'. It's a day where ex-adult stars came in and talked about how their life had been changed by church and God," she said. During her time volunteering, Ashley Graham was working in the elevator during the church event where she was "welcoming people, passing out candy and pushing the button to the eighth floor," she revealed.

Everything changed for Ashley when Justin Ervin walked into the elevator with a friend. It was clear both of them were interested, however, Ervin took the first shot by introducing himself. The two instantly fell in love, leaving Ashley to share that she felt mesmerized when they had first met, claiming, "he seemed to be looking into my soul when we talked," she said.

The couple didn't waste any time when it came to their romance. Following their nuptials in 2010, Justin and Ashley focused on their respective careers, that is until they welcomed their baby boy, Isaac Ervin, in 2020. This past January, Justin, and Ashley celebrated Isaac's 1st birthday, showcasing just how beautiful a family they really are.

What Does Justin Ervin Do?

While fans know Justin Ervin has a great husband and even better father, they have always been curious about what he does outside of his relationship with Ashley. Well, much like Graham, Justin is also in the entertainment business.

Ervin is quite the successful cinematographer, director, producer, and writer, making him a true quadruple threat! Justin has worked on an array of projects, which includes his time working alongside Ashley and her many fashion campaigns. Justin Ervin landed contracts with Netflix, Adobe, Guess, Pantene, BET, The Gap, and CNN, to name a few.

The 33-year-old specialized in documentary filmmaking, which he earned a degree in from the School of Visual Arts in New York. Justin has since screened his many films at countless festivals across the globe, proving that he's just as much a success as Ashley is.
