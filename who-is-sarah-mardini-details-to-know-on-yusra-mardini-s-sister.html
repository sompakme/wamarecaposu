Sarah Mardini is an activist who is fighting for human rights. Let’s know in detail about Yusra Mardini’s sister in this article.

Sarah Mardini is one of the 6.7 million Syrian refugees who have been forced to escape their homeland.

Sarah and her sister Yusra risked their lives to safeguard 18 other immigrants on their trek to Europe in 2015.

They spent eight months in a refugee camp in Berlin before being granted asylum.

Who Is Sarah Mardini’s Sister?

Yusra Mardini is a Syrian swimmer presently based in Hamburg, Germany.

She was a part of the Olympic Refugee Athlete Team (ROT).

On April 27, 2017, Mardini was designated a UNHCR Goodwill Ambassador.

She also competed for the Refugee Olympic Team in the Tokyo 2020 Summer Olympics.
