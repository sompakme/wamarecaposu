n. 1. a cardinal number, ten times nine. 2. a symbol for this number, as 90 or XC.

Which is correct ninety or ninty?

Ninety is the written form of the number 90, such that it is one more than 89 and one less than 91. Ninety is the correct spelling. Ninty is a misspelling.
