Bow Wow Wants To Fulfill A Childhood Dream By Winning The WWE Tag Titles With Rey Mysterio

 Posted on September 5, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  325 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Never say never — wants to make his dream of being a tag team champion come true.

The hip-hop star and actor says he has a dream of winning gold in WWE, and he plans on doing just that once he releases his final album.

Now i know this might sound crazy… BUT… after i drop my last album. I will focus on tv and film. And joining the @WWE its been a life long childhood dream to wrestle in the WWE!!!!

Me and @reymysterio vs whoever @WWE for the tag team titles…

It’s not out of the ordinary to see hip-hop icons show up in the pro wrestling world, as SHAQ is set to make his AEW in-ring debut next month and Bad Bunny is rumored to be wrestling a tag team match at WrestleMania 37.

There are a few issues with Bow Wow’s plan, however, as he wants to team with Rey Mysterio, who already has a tag partner in his son, Dominik. In addition, Cedric Alexander and Shelton Benjamin of The Hurt Business and Dolph Ziggler & Robert Roode are the respective tag champs on RAW and SmackDown, so if he plans to be a champion soon, he (and Rey) would have to go through one of those teams.

Bow Wow officially announced he was retiring from rapping in 2016 and had planned on calling his final album NYLTH, but it has still not been released. As of late, Bow Wow has been building his acting resume, appearing on Growing Up Hip Hop: Atlanta and on The Masked Singer as The Frog. He is scheduled to appear as “Twinkie” in the ninth film in the Fast And The Furious franchise, F9. 

Now i know this might sound crazy… BUT… after i drop my last album. I will focus on tv and film. And joining the @WWE its been a life long childhood dream to wrestle in the WWE!!!!

— Bow Wow (@smoss) February 8, 2021
