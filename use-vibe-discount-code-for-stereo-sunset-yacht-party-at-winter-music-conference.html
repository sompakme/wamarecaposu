Throughout the past decade, Chus and Ceballos have been the pioneers and creators of the underground movement known as the ‘Iberican Sound’. Hailed worldwide for their distinctive blend of styles between house and techno, the duo hits Miami for this year’s WMC celebration by headlining at the Stereo Sunset Yacht Party (presented by Stereo Productions). Chus and Ceballos will also be joined by fellow label artists Patrick M, Anthony Attalla and RJ Pickens while cruising and boozing along the beautiful cool waters of the Gold Coast.
