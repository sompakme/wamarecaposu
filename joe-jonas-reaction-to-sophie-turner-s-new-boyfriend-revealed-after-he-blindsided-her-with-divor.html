Joe Jonas may have been the one to end his marriage to Sophie Turner, but the Game of Thrones actress has made it clear she’s moving on, as she’s been spotted getting close to British aristocrat Peregrine "Perry" Pearson.

Many fans are anxious to know more about Sophie’s new romance, including how her estranged husband Joe Jonas feels, as they navigate their ongoing divorce.

Sophie was first linked to Perry in October, when they were spotted in Paris, only to later be photographed attending the Rugby World Cup Final match between New Zealand and South Africa at Stade de France shortly later. Paparazzi photos have showcased the pair kissing, confirming their romantic status.

One Report Claimed Joe Was “Livid” Over Sophie’s New Romance

Initially, it was reported that Joe was upset seeing Sophie move on with someone new, especially in such a public fashion. “Joe is livid with the latest coming from Sophie, making him look like a horrible father,” a source told Daily Mail. “'It is disgusting in his eyes what she has chosen to do.”

“This is only going to be the start of stuff getting messier and messier and messier,” they went on. “Joe thought they were moving in a forward direction with the divorce and finding a way to co-parent but now it is just a nasty mess, and the latest lawsuit threw any type of positivity all off course.”

The insider said that Joe sees their divorce as growing only more contentious due to Sophie’s public actions. “Frustration is the emotion running through him right now and it doesn't seem like that will be changing anytime soon.”

A second source told Daily Mail that Joe is concerned about the harm the divorce will have on their two young daughters, aged 3 years old and 14 months. He also apparently believes Sophie’s recent outing with Taylor Swift have been staged.

“The kids are the victims,” the insider revealed. “Sophie and Taylor are not close. Joe took Sophie to a show of Taylor's, and it wasn't even comped. Again, he is who filed for divorce. Read between the line.”

Joe’s Representative Denies The Allegations Are True

However, Joe claims the reports aren’t true. In fact, a representative for the musician have a simple statement to Radar Online about the allegations, saying, “It’s completely not true.”

Sophie hasn’t directly commented on her budding relationship with Perry. But a report from earlier this month says the actress is just getting back into the dating game, and her new romance may only be casual.

“Sophie is open to the idea of dating again,” a source told Us Weekly. “She isn’t fully committed to anybody at the moment, but she’s definitely putting herself out there and keeping her options open.”

“[They] are not serious at all” the insider continued, adding that Sophie is “casually dating a few people” and “keeping her options open.”

“There are a few men she’s shown interest in but [Sophie] isn’t jumping into anything or settling down,” they added.

Joe filed for divorce in September, only for Sophie to sue him for allegedly withholding their kids’ passports. The parents-of-two reached a temporary custody agreement last month as they continue to settle the terms of their divorce.
