Billie Eilish's upcoming album Happier Than Ever is ushering in a new era for the global superstar. Ever since Eilish's incredible Vogue photoshoot, fans have praised the singer for inspiring them by being comfortable in her body. They've defended her right to bear more skin and are loving the dance moves she has displayed in her newest music video!

In a clip shared by the Grammy-winning singer to TikTok, Eilish and her girl squad are seen dancing to the choreography for Lost Cause. The singer replaced her usual baggy clothes for a silk gray romper featuring a plunging neckline, wore a brown hat with 'B' imprinted on it and threw on an oversized brown jacket.
