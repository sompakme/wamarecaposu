Three-Day Passes to Summer Set Music Festival

 Posted on September 16, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  198 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

August is the month to party. Did you not know that? VIBE wants to help you help yourself get your dance on this summer. We are giving away two pairs of three-day passes to the Summer Set Music and Camping Festival in Somerset, Wisconsin — a village some 3,000 people strong nestled along the Cheese State’s Apple River.

Between August 9-11 this little ol’ town known for its curds and Green Bay Packers pride is going to go off with the help of dance-prone acts Big Gigantic, Girl Talk, Passion Pit, STS9, and our boys Big Boi and Common. Diplo will be there to Instagram all the twerking, as will Krewella, Wolfgang Gartner, Zedd, Madeon, Polica, SBTRKT, STRFKR, Tommy Trash and more.

Are you going already? Whaaaat? You need a ticket? We got you covered.

Enter to win the passes by commenting on this here post or retweeting the link with the hashtag #vibesummerset to let us know why you deserve to be there.
