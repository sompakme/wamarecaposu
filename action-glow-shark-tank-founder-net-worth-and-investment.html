What is Action Glow?

Action Glow is a company that specializes in manufacturing innovative LED lighting systems for various sporting equipment. Whether you’re hitting the slopes, riding the waves, or cruising the streets, Action Glow’s cutting-edge products are designed to enhance your experience by providing exceptional style and safety.

Our LED lighting systems are specifically crafted for popular sporting equipment such as snowboards, skis, surfboards, skateboards, and kayaks. These high-quality lights can be easily attached using a strong adhesive, ensuring a secure fit even during extreme activities.

Powered by a reliable 9-volt battery, our LED lights offer long-lasting illumination, allowing you to pursue your passion without worrying about your visibility in low-light conditions. With Action Glow, you can confidently take on thrilling adventures day or night.

What sets Action Glow apart from the competition is our commitment to quality and innovation. We have patented our unique attachment method for LED lighting systems, ensuring that our products are perfectly tailored to your sporting equipment.

Pricing for our LED lighting systems varies depending on the type of equipment, ranging from $69 to $169. Our products are designed to withstand the demands of action sports, providing exceptional durability and performance.

Founders of Action Glow

Action Glow was founded by brothers Garret Porter and Dakota Porter. With a shared passion for action sports and innovative lighting technology, they embarked on a journey to create the best action sport lighting system in the market. Drawing inspiration from their own experiences, the brothers came up with the idea for LED lighting systems when they illuminated their snowboarding equipment and captured the attention of fellow snowboarders.

For over a decade, Garret and Dakota Porter have been dedicated to transforming the way people approach sports at night. They have poured their hearts and souls into building Action Glow into a reputable brand that offers not only style but also safety to athletes worldwide.

Action Glow Shark Tank Pitch

In their quest for funding to expand their business, Garret and Dakota Porter took their innovative LED lighting system to the renowned entrepreneurship show Shark Tank. Season 14 of Shark Tank provided the perfect platform for the founders of Action Glow to showcase their product and secure the investment they needed.

Appearing on Shark Tank Season 14, Garret and Dakota Porter pitched their business in front of the show’s panel of esteemed investors, including the renowned entrepreneur Robert Herjavec. The brothers sought a $200,000 investment in exchange for a 15% equity stake in Action Glow.

Robert Herjavec recognized the potential of Action Glow and made an initial offer of $200,000 for a 30% stake in the company. After careful consideration, Garret and Dakota Porter accepted the offer, acknowledging the value that Robert Herjavec’s experience and expertise would bring to their business.

Robert Herjavec’s initial offer of $200,000 for a 30% equity stake in Action Glow was eagerly accepted by Garret and Dakota Porter after their impressive pitch on Shark Tank Season 14.

The founders had the opportunity to showcase their groundbreaking LED lighting system during their presentation on Shark Tank. The pitch emphasized the unique features and benefits of Action Glow, highlighting the product’s popularity and success in the market.

Through the power of viral marketing, Action Glow had successfully captured the attention of consumers and generated a significant number of sales. The founders showcased these achievements to the Shark Tank panel, demonstrating the strong potential for further growth and success.

The investment secured from Robert Herjavec has allowed Action Glow to further expand its operations and continue innovating in the LED lighting industry. This partnership has positioned the company for continued success and cemented its status as a key player in the market.
