Miley Cyrus may have been famous since she was a teen, but Liam Hemsworth had no idea who she was when they first met. Hemsworth has explained that he had no idea how famous his co-star was when they met on the set of The Last Song. Prior to filming the romantic adaptation, Cyrus had found huge international success appearing as Hannah Montana.

Cyrus and Hemsworth met in 2009 on the set of the Nicholas Sparks adaptation. Although the young actors weren’t looking for love during filming, sparks flew. Shortly after filming wrapped, the pair confirmed their romance with a PDA-filled trip through an airport.

After years of being on and off, they split in 2013. In 2016, they reunited and in December 2018, the two married in a quiet ceremony. Sadly, the pair split just a year later with Miley revealing, "What really sucked about it wasn't the fact that me and someone that I loved realized that we don't love each other the way that we used to anymore."

It appears Cyrus and Hemsworth didn’t have a lot in common aside from a shared career. “That was really traumatizing,” Miley told Alexandra Cooper on the Call Her Daddy podcast in regard to the breakdown of her marriage. Cyrus added, “It’s like a death when you lose a love that deep,” but she looked fondly on their time together, “The relationship that I had for 10 years was an amazing time in my life.”

Liam Hemsworth Had No Idea Who Miley Cyrus Was When Making The Last Song

Liam Hemsworth met his future ex-wife Miley Cyrus on the set of The Last Song, but he had no idea how big of a star she was before being cast.

"I grew up outside of Melbourne on an island that was connected to the mainland by a bridge. My brothers and I would surf and live on the beach," the Australian actor explained.

Why Liam Hemsworth Almost Wasn't Cast In The Last Song Opposite Miley Cyrus

During a 2019 interview with Today Liam Hemsworth revealed he almost didn’t get the part as Will Blakelee in The Last Song — the role that introduced him to his on/off partner of a decade.

“That was the first job I got [in the U.S.], and it was literally right at the end of my three-month visa,” Hemsworth explained. “They cast another kid, and it didn’t work out with him.”

Miley Cyrus And Liam Hemsworth Opened Up About Falling In Love On Set

Liam Hemsworth may not have realized how famous Miley Cyrus was, but Hemsworth was smitten with the singer/actress. They were just 17 and 19 when they first met and fell in love on set.
